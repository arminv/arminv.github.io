Welcome to my page!


My name is Armin Varshokar and I am an aspiring software developer based in Toronto, Canada. My go-to language for general scripting is Python but I also use R for data science/statistical tasks. Please check out both my GitHub and CodePen pages to see some of my projects.


I am currently learning web development by taking online courses on platforms such as freeCodeCamp and Udemy. Another great learning platform that I use for data science is DataCamp. I truly enjoy the freedom of self-paced learning that online courses offer.


So far I have learned HTML/CSS and right now I am working on pure JavaScript. I especially enjoy learning about subtle nuances between Python and JavaScript and I plan to further my knowledge of both languages by documenting such differences as a project.


I have a finance background and in addition to programming I am also working on the CFA program. My long-term goal is to be able to apply the software tools I am learning about to finance.


I am a big fan of tennis and basketball. I play a lot of tennis during summer but unfortunately I don't get to play basketball as often as I would like!
